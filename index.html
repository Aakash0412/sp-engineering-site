ABOUT SP ENGINEERING

Industrial field expertise with a safety-first mindset.

SP Engineering is a specialised industrial service provider focused on industrial elevator erection, EOT crane installation and maintenance, and associated electrical works. We work closely with OEMs and plant engineering teams to ensure safe, reliable and standards-compliant operations.

From new projects to retrofits and annual maintenance contracts, our team combines strong technical competence with disciplined on-site execution. We support plants across manufacturing, chemicals, process industry and other heavy-duty environments.

What we are known for

  • Standards-compliant elevator & crane installations.
  • Measured, safe execution at site.
  • Structured AMC & responsive breakdown support.

Industries we support

  • Refineries & process plants
  • Chemical & agrochemical units
  • Manufacturing facilities
  • Large industrial campuses

Execution principles

  • • Zero-compromise approach to safety and compliance.
  • • Detailed planning before every erection or shutdown activity.
  • • Documentation, checklists and OEM-guided procedures.
  • • Transparent communication with plant engineering teams.
